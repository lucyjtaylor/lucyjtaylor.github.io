Increasing audience engagement for F1

At Flamingo Digital Consultancy, I delivered research for Formula 1 recommending that the sport could grow its audiences by focussing on the drivers and the drama, not just the cars.

5 years later in 2022 the popularity of Netflix’s ‘Drive to Survive’ and the resulting boom in Formula 1 fans is evidence that these recommendations were bang on the money.

Using Google Trends, Facebook audience insights, Picodash, Keyhole, and Answer the public, I uncovered patterns of current and emerging user behaviours and built recommendations of how to exploit these insights to grow audiences.

Times change, and while I recommended using Snapchat as a tool for short form video engagement, TikTok has since conquered all. Formula 1 themed driver videos are notably popular:
